#Thebot - A Twitter Bot

#Thebot is a Twitter Bot which produces and sends humorous tweets. #Thebot currently tweets around twice a day. Each tweet is appends a small phrase to the beginning of a noun to produce a humorous hashtag.

This bot was developed in Python 3.6 and utilizes the Twitter API through the Tweepy library. The bot will tweet until it has iterated through the entire list of nouns. Currently, it is hosted on PythonAnywhere.

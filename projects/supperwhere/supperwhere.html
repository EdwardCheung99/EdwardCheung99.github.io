Supperwhere - A Restaurant Application

Supperwhere is an application that allows users to figure out dining recommendations based on their dietary preferences and meal history. The app helps users locate restaurants for the user based on those preferences. By providing dining suggestions, Supperwhere makes the dining experience quicker and less indecisive.

Users can adjust their preferences based on their preferred cuisines and price range. Based on these preferences, the user's location, and the meals that the user has eaten, Supperwhere will automatically recommend restaurants to the user's profile. Supperwhere also allows users to freely search for restaurants, if they still wish to find restaurants on their own.
